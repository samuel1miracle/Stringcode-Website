Our world has evolved; traditional methods of problem solving are now obsolete, giving way to Information Technology as the modern means of solution proffering.

Therefore, to have proper functionality, and a chance to expand our dealings to a global scope in the modern world, the need to do all operations via IT cannot be overemphasized.

With this in mind, Stringcode Limited, an Information Technology firm based in the heart of the city of Port Harcourt, Rivers State, Nigeria, was borne on the 11th of June, 2019 with Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) Registration Number: 1591402

Our sole aim is to use the Knowledge of software to proffer lasting solutions to traditional ways of doing things.

We started as a small company but with determination and consistency, we have grown to be a well-known IT company in the city of Port Harcourt and we have handled projects for the Rivers state government, individuals and some reputable companies such as Heneve Estate, Goddypee Logistics and more.
